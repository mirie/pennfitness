Penn Fitness Help Page

Welcome to PennFitness!

This website allows you to map your exercise routes in Philadelphia. You can also create Events (associated with a particular route) and create Groups.

To Begin:

  1. Sign up for an account: Click on Sign Up! in the upper right menu bar and fill out the fields to register. Note: Height, Weight, and Gender are optional.
  2. Once you successfully register, you can login :)

Upper Menu Bar Options:

Once you login, you will be able to access the following additional options: My Account and Create a New Route.

My Account

When you click on this link, a dialog containing all of your information will be loaded. This includes your personal information, your associated Groups, your Events, and your Routes.

Personal Information

This is where you can modify your personal information that you entered when you first registered. Click on this tab to refresh your personal information.

Group Information

This tab has the following 3 sub-tabs: My Groups, Created Groups, and Create a Group

My Groups

Click on this tab to refresh its contents. This tab displays all of the Groups you've subscribed to. You can select a particular Group and click Unsubscribe to leave the Group.

Created Groups

Click on this tab to refresh its contents. This tab displays all of the Groups (and their details) that you've created. You can delete a Group or Send emails to the subscibed members of a group by after selecting its associated radio button.


Event Information

This tab has the following 2 sub-tabs: Registered Events and Created Events

Registered Events

Click on this tab to refresh its contents. This tab displays all of the Events you've registered to. Unsubscribing from an Event is currently not implemented

Create Events

Click on this tab to refresh its contents. This tab displays all of the Events you've created.


Route Information

Click on this tab to refresh its contents. This tab displays all of the Routes you've created.

Create a New Route

This link brings up a dialog to create a route. You can click on the map to draw the route. If you want to view the virtual trip for the route, you must save the route first (you can always modify it later).

After you save a route, you will notice that you can create an Event for the route and also rate it. Note: You can only rate a particular route ONCE! You can also delete a route if no events are associated with it. A route can have many Events associated with it.

Left MenuBar:

  • The Events and Routes menu options expand/shrink to display/hide additional information. Click on 'Events' or 'Routes' to see tabs containing the information. You can click on 'Events' or 'Routes' to collapse the menu.
  • The Calendar highlights in green the days that have Events associated with them. Click on a green day to load the list of events below the calendar.

Other Notes:

  • You may Register for an Event once. The Register option is available when you are viewing the Event details.
